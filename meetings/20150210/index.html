Webtuesday February: Contenteditable with Editable.JS

Editable.JS is the friendly contenteditable API. In this talk you will learn the basics of contenteditable and how you can use Editable.JS to make your sites editable in the browser. I'll also explain how we use it within Livingdocs to handle complex scenarios.

About Lukas Peyer

Lukas is a co-founder at upfront.io developing the next-generation publishing CMS livingdocs which is currently used at watson.ch and the NZZ.
